Sirowa Sacco

Our promise at Sirowa Sacco to our 500,000 members is to provide you with financial freedom in form of a 3 bedroomed apartment in one of the four counties in Luo land and KES. 20K monthly passive income from dividends within the next 7 years.

Your duty is to deposit the required share capital of KES. 5,000 to be a fully paid member, the KES. 1,200 registration, and the monthly minimum shareholding of KES. 100 per share.
